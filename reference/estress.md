# ESTRESS data

ESTRESS data

## Usage

``` r
estress
```

## Format

### `estress`

A data frame with 7 variables, 262 cases:

- tenure:

  Company Tenure

- estress:

  Economic stress

- affect:

  Depressed affect

- withdraw:

  Withdrawal intentions

- sex:

  Male (1) or Female (0)

- age:

  age

- ese:

  Entrepreneurial self efficacy

## Source

Pollack, J., VanEpps, E. M., & Hayes, A. F. (2012). The moderating role
of social ties on entrepreneurs' depressed affect and withdrawal
intentions in response to economic stress. Journal of Organizational
Behavior, 33, 789-810.

Hayes, A. (2017). Introduction to mediation, moderation, and conditional
process analysis: A regression-based approach (2nd edn, p. 692).
Guilford Press.
